What are we all about?

We think your room should inspire you and reflect your passions, and we're making it easier than ever. Buy a poster from us for $15 and you can trade it for another one, as often as you want, for free. We'll even deliver the new one and provide putty to put it up and take it down without ripping.

Questions?
  • Why should I buy from Roominate?

    We provide the opportunity to experience the awesome-factor of many posters for the price of one poster!

  • What if I don't have time to decorate?

    We'll deliver your poster when you want and we'll even help put it up!

  • How often can I trade for a new poster?

    As often as you want, provided it is in good condition with no tearing.

  • How big are the posters?

    Most of our posters are 24 by 36 inches. Some are slightly different proportions but similar sizes.

  • How long before I get the poster?

    We'll deliver as fast as we can. You'll likely receive your poster less than two days from when you order it, pending setting up a time for us to deliver it.
